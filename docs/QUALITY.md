# Quality scorecard

Grade each product domain quarterly and after major incidents. Link evidence; do not assign aspirational grades.

| Domain | Correctness | Tests | Observability | Docs | Security | Owner | Next gap |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Example domain | Not graded | Not graded | Not graded | Not graded | Not graded | Unassigned | Establish baseline |

Grades use `A` (strong and evidenced), `B` (adequate), `C` (material gap), or `D` (unsafe/unreliable). Convert repeated or high-risk gaps into active execution plans or tracked debt.
